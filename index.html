ANA (Alpha Numeric Augmenter) modifies an existing wordlist to include 'munged passwords', such as when letters are replaced with similar-looking numbers. Wordlists are augmented according to the specified substitutions: 5 -> S, 4 -> A, B -> 8, etc. (configurable).
